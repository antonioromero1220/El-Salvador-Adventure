Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book your adventure with us directly on our website. Fill out your preferred tour package, choose your dates, and fill out the form to confirm the booking. Our customer service team will then reach out to confirm the booking details and give exact pricing.

What types of tours are available?

We offer a wide range of tours to suit every adventurer's need. From cultural and historical tours exploring ancient ruins to nature treks through lush forests and adventures on renowned beaches, we have something for everyone.

Can I customize my tour package?

Absolutely! We specialize in personalized tour packages. Let us know what you're interested in, and we'll tailor a tour that perfectly matches your adventure preferences.

What is included in the tour package?

All our tour packages include transportation on our premium buses, guided tours by our expert guides. The exact details of your specific tour package will be confirmed after speaking to our customer service and confirming your personalized tour.

What should I bring on my tour?

We recommend bringing comfortable clothing suitable for the climate, sturdy walking shoes, a hat, sunscreen, a water bottle, and a camera to capture your adventures. Specific tours may have additional recommendations, which we'll provide upon booking.

Are the tours suitable for children?

Yes, we offer family-friendly tours suitable for children. Please let us know the ages of the children when booking so we can ensure the activities are appropriate for them.

What safety measures are in place?

Your safety is our top priority. We operate with experienced drivers and guides, and our buses are equipped with modern safety features, including cameras in every bus. We closely monitor all tour locations for safety and adjust itineraries as needed.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made up to 3 days before the tour date. For more details, please contact our customer service.

How can I contact customer service?

Our customer service team is available to assist you with any questions or concerns. You can reach us via email, phone, or through our WhatsApp.
